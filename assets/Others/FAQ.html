Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to setup a server?

  • A device that can run the Palworld Server software.
  • An internet connection with enough bandwith for your users.
  • A single user will require around 1Mbps of upload and download.
  • If you plan on having multiple users, you should have at least 5Mbps available.
